Colorado and New Mexico Roadtrip!

Day 0 - 9/17

I took a flight from Seattle to Albuquerque to meet up with my parents and dog to start the trip!

Day 1 - 9/18

On the first full day of traveling, we drove a loop in northern New Mexico all the way into the southern portion of Colorado. Our notable stops were in Abiquiu, Chama, Antonito, and Taos. In Abiquiu, NM, we saw some of the rock formations that inspired Georgia O'Keeffe's paintings on the Ghost Ranch. In Chama, NM, we saw an interesting Halloween display. This is a really small town that is home to one end of the Cumbres and Toltec Scenic Railroad. After stopping in Chama, NM, we drove through La Manga Pass through Antonito, CO for lunch. Then we headed down to Taos! I took Izzy over the Rio Grande Gorge Bridge in Taos, NM. This is always a beautiful sight.

Day 2 - 9/19

On the second day, I started the day with a nice run down the Santa Fe River Trail. Afterwards, we drove to Los Alamos up a cliffside and were greeted with these awesome views. Then, we drove through the Valles Caldera National Preserve, which is in the center of a 13.7 mile wide volcanic caldera. Longmire fans will recognize the scenery as where parts of the show take place. For lunch, we went to a small bakery in Jemez Springs named Highway 4 Cafe & Bakery. They had awesome coffee, green chili breakfast burritos, and cheesecake. After lunch, we went to a cool spot called the Gilman Tunnels where two holes have been blasted in the rock. I believe it used to be for trains to travel through. On the way back to Santa Fe, we got to stop by Bandelier! We ended the day with this beautiful sunset.
